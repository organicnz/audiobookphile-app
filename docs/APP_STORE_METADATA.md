## Description

Audiobookphile is the ultimate, free, open-source companion app for your self-hosted Audiobookshelf server. We threw out the standard templates and engineered a truly premium, native iOS experience that looks and feels like a first-party Apple application. 

**LIQUID GLASS AESTHETICS**
Experience an entirely new visual paradigm. Our custom Liquid Glass engine dynamically extracts the rich color palettes from your audiobook covers and generates a breathtaking, fluidly animated aura that physically responds to your scrolling. 

**iOS 27 NATIVE DESIGN**
Audiobookphile is built exclusively using the latest cutting-edge Swift and iOS frameworks:
• **Dynamic Toolbars**: Your iPhone's status bar dynamically inverts its colors to guarantee perfect contrast against your book covers.
• **Cascading Bookshelf**: Scroll through your library and watch your books physically cascade into view in 3D. 
• **SF Symbol Animations**: Buttons morph, bounce, and react to your taps with hardware-accelerated animations and declarative haptics. 

**POWERFUL FEATURES**
• Native AirPlay Casting support for HomePods and Apple TVs
• Native Siri Integration ("Hey Siri, play my book in Audiobookphile")
• Smart HTTP Caching for ultra-fast, offline-capable browsing
• Sleep Timer & Custom Playback Speeds
• Deep Lock Screen & Command Center integration

Audiobookphile is 100% free, forever. No subscriptions, no ads, no paywalls. Just your books, beautifully presented.
